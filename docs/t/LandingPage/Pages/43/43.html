Degree of protection

Engineered for the toughest conditions: Elecq charging stations come with an IP65 protection rating, offering superior resistance to dust and water. While others settle for IP54, we ensure your charging experience remains reliable-no matter the weather.

IP 65

  • Ideal for outdoor installations exposed to harsh conditions.
  • Better suited for environments with high dust levels and exposure to rain or cleaning processes involving water jets.

IP 54

  • Suitable for indoor use or sheltered outdoor areas.
  • Adequate in environments with minimal dust and limited exposure to water splashes.

Relay selection complies with IEC61851.1 standard

Relay selection complies with the IEC61851.1-2017 standard, while many competitors do not.

- Elecq's Zettler AZSR131-1AE-12D meets the IEC61851.1-2017 standard's requirement for a minimum contact gap of 3mm, whereas Brand E's AZSR131-1AE-12DGW(200) does not.
- Elecq meets the IEC61851-1-2017 requirement for 50,000 electrical life cycles, while Brand E does not.
- Elecq meets the 3KA short-circuit withstand current, complying with the IEC62955 RDC MD short-circuit capability requirement, while Brand E does not.

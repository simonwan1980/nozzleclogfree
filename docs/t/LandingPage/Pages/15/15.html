Protocol

Direct OCPP

ELECQ’s full range of chargers is built on a Direct OCPP + Elecq Service Protocol architecture, featuring cloud-native redundancy and local network outage buffering.

The system supports fault-tolerant capabilities such as resumable transmission, message retry, and session persistence.

In typical commercial deployments, it achieves an annual uptime of 99.99% (less than 1 hour of downtime per year), outperforming the industry average and meeting the EU’s high-availability standards for public EV charging infrastructure.

OCPP stack OTA upgradability

ELECQ chargers are equipped with a powerful SoC and a modular OCPP stack architecture that supports full OTA (Over-the-Air) upgrades — from OCPP 1.6 to 2.0.1 and now to OCPP 2.1.

While many legacy chargers require complete hardware replacement to adopt newer OCPP versions, ELECQ enables protocol upgrades remotely — saving time, cost, and operational effort.

Invest once, scale continuously. With ELECQ, your infrastructure stays compliant, future-ready, and aligned with evolving industry standards — without disruption to service or users.

ELECQ is not just another charger. It’s a smart, upgradeable platform built for tomorrow.

*The Open Charge Alliance released OCPP 2.1 on January 23, 2025.

Pre-mount with Elecq Ready

Elecq Ready allows you to pre-mount the backplate for easy deployment without needing an electrician during actual use. This is the perfect choice, especially since the revised Energy Performance of Buildings Directive (EPBD) came into force, requiring pre-cabling for at least 50% of parking spaces.

The Energy Performance of Buildings Directive (EPBD)：

  • New buildings and major renovations (residential and non-residential) must include pre-cabling (installation of conduits for electrical wiring) to facilitate the future installation of EV charging points.
  • Residential buildings with more than 10 parking spaces must ensure every parking space is pre-cabled.
  • Non-residential buildings with more than 10 parking spaces must install at least one charging point and pre-cable for 20% of parking spaces.
  • From 2025, all existing non-residential buildings with more than 20 parking spaces must install at least one charging point.
  • Charging points must support smart charging capabilities, enabling dynamic energy management and integration with renewable energy sources.
  • Member States are required to incentivize the installation of EV charging points and pre-cabling during building renovations.

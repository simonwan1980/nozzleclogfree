Dual channel connection

Supports Elecq service channel connection and direct OCPP connection at the same time.

When OCPP directly connect to CPO's platform, the service channel can still connect to Elecq cloud. Both Elecq and the CSMS Platform can find the error code.The service channel can implement remote diagnostic, this make it easier to tacking down problems.

Dual-platform EV chargers supporting direct OCPP provide enhanced flexibility, compatibility, and stability, delivering greater convenience for both users and operators.

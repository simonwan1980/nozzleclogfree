Master Charger Mode

Any Elecq charger can operate in master mode, enabling self-networking management and dynamic load balancing (DLB) for up to 30 chargers without additional equipment.


For larger setups exceeding 30 units, self-networking and load balancing for up to 500 chargers can be achieved with the help of master charger.

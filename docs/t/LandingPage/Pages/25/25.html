Charging Modes

By using Elecq Power Monitor alongside Elecq Home, you can easily charge with solar power via our app to save on your energy bill.


Select the best charging mode based on your electricity plan: Only Solar, Solar Priority or Unlimited, to balance charging efficiency with charging cost.

Only Solar
The Elecq chargers exclusively use solar power for charging, without drawing any electricity from the grid.
Solar Priority
Prioritize using solar power for charging. If the solar output is insufficient, the charger will operate at the minimum power (6A). If there is enough solar energy, it will rely solely on that, without drawing from the grid.
Unlimited
Prioritize using solar power, with the grid supplying any shortfall. In this case, the charger will still operate at its maximum power output.

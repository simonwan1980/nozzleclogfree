Protocol

ISO 15118-20 / OCPP / PLC

Elecq is equipped with built-in PLC, supporting the ISO 15118-20 protocol, enabling V2H (Vehicle-to-Home) and V2G (Vehicle-to-Grid) and state of charge capabilities.

Elecq is fully compatible with OCPP 2.0.1 and OCPP 1.6J.

Elecq is equipped with built-in PLC, enabling Plug & Charge.
